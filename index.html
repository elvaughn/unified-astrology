About Unified Astrology

Astrology is fundamentally a metaphysical study of reality and the mechanics which give rise to it. Our interpretation of astrological elements depends on our understanding of their role as generators of Existence, and our ability to follow their manifestations throughout various realms, translating information as relevant to the subject of analysis.

Unified Astrology opens our view to numerous realms of manifestation and dimensions of the chart, giving the student a variety of viewpoints and abundant means to interpret the horoscope.

At the core, Unified Astrology is distinctly a Hermetic art, with a comprehension of reality based on the Hermetic Principles and conjured from meaningful contemplation on The Emerald Tablets, which by the exalted wisdom of their author have inspired the inception of this work.

Study Unified Astrology

When studied and applied correctly, Unified Astrology can serve as an invaluable tool for gaining clarity on one’s path and purpose, identifying the foundation of one’s challenges and opportunities, navigating the tides of life and making well timed decisions.

The study and practice of Unified Astrology takes place within a well-defined frame, respecting the natural order of things and making sure to consolidate each step, to ensure that the student obtains a sophisticated grasp of astrology and an effective approach to astrological interpretation.
